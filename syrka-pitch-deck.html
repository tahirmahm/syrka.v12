We take a nation's vision — Saudi Vision 2030, Malta Vision 2050 — and map the delta between where they are today and where they need to be. AI against government and OECD data identifies exactly which skills the nation lacks, sector by sector. Those signals flow into universities, optimising curricula so every graduating cohort is calibrated to what the economy actually needs. The placement layer connects high performing students directly to employers in national priority sectors.
