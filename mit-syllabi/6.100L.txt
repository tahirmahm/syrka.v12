=== COURSE DESCRIPTION ===
This course is aimed at students with little or no programming experience. It aims to provide students with an understanding of the role computation can play in solving problems and to help students, regardless of their major, feel justifiably confident of their ability to write small programs that allow them to accomplish useful goals. The class uses the Python 3 programming language.

The course covers the fundamentals of programming, computational problem solving, data structures, algorithms, and introduces key concepts in computer science. Students learn to think computationally and develop programs to solve real-world problems.
