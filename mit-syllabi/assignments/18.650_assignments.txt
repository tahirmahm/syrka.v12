=== SKILLS DEMONSTRATED ===
- Derive method of moments estimators for standard distributions
- Compute MLE analytically and verify asymptotic properties
- Prove consistency and asymptotic normality of MLE
- Calculate Fisher information and verify Cramér-Rao bound
- Design hypothesis tests with specified Type I error rate
- Compute p-values and interpret statistical significance
- Perform chi-squared and Kolmogorov-Smirnov goodness-of-fit tests
- Fit OLS regression: estimate coefficients, compute R², diagnose residuals
- Apply Bayesian inference: conjugate priors, compute posteriors analytically
- Reduce dimensionality with PCA: eigenvectors, explained variance ratio
- Fit logistic regression and interpret log-odds, predicted probabilities
- Apply Poisson regression for count data
- Use R or Python for statistical computing
