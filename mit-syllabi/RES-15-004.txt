=== COURSE DESCRIPTION ===
This course introduces students to system dynamics and systems thinking, providing tools for understanding the structure and dynamics of complex systems. Students learn to identify feedback loops, delays, and nonlinearities that drive system behaviour; construct causal loop diagrams and stock-and-flow models; and simulate system behaviour over time. The course uses real-world examples from business, public health, ecology, and technology adoption.

Systems thinking enables students to see interconnections rather than linear cause-and-effect chains, an essential skill for managing complexity in AI deployment, organisational design, and policy analysis.
