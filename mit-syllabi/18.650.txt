MIT 18.650 — Statistics for Applications
Source: MIT OpenCourseWare
URL: https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/18-650-statistics-for-applications-fall-2016/

=== COURSE DESCRIPTION ===
This course offers an in-depth treatment of the fundamentals of statistical methods and their applications. Topics include parametric and non-parametric statistics, estimation, confidence intervals, hypothesis testing, regression analysis, and Bayesian statistics. The emphasis is on understanding the mathematical foundations of statistical methods and applying them to real data.

The course bridges theoretical statistics with practical data analysis, preparing students to apply rigorous statistical reasoning to problems in science, engineering, and data science.
